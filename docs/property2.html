Property Description

Experience the pinnacle of luxury living in this magnificent 5-bedroom home nestled within the prestigious Simbithi Eco Estate. This architectural masterpiece seamlessly blends contemporary design with sustainable living principles.

The grand entrance leads to expansive open-plan living areas with soaring ceilings and floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the lush indigenous gardens. The gourmet kitchen features premium finishes, a central island, and top-of-the-line appliances.

The luxurious master suite occupies the entire upper level, complete with a private balcony, walk-in dressing room, and spa-like en-suite. Four additional bedrooms, each with en-suite bathrooms, provide comfort for family and guests.

The outdoor entertainment area features a sparkling pool, built-in braai, and covered patio overlooking the meticulously landscaped gardens. Additional features include a triple garage, wine cellar, and home office.
